What is Entertainment Expenses

Type 1: Qualified entertainment expenses
  • Theaters
  • Social, athletic, and sporting clubs
  • Sporting events
  • Hunting, fishing, vacation and similar types of trips
  • Cost of meals during entertainment
  • Electronic games

3 Ways to Reduce Entertainment Expenses

Method 1: Budgeting At-Home Entertainment Expenses
  • Purchase a cable alternative
  • Listen to music via streaming services
  • Acquire a library card
  • Rent video games instead of buying them
  • Split entertainment memberships with your loved ones
Method 2: Accessing Local Low-Budget Entertainment
  • Check out student, senior, or other discounts
  • Choose matinees over evening movies
  • Watch for free community events
  • Watch for discount days or happy hours
  • Buy season passes to your favorite places
  • Volunteer at special events
Method 3: Spending Less on Vacation Entertainment
  • Travel during your destination's off-season
  • Buy event or plane tickets several months in advance
  • Purchase group deals online
  • Pack plenty of water and snacks
  • Spend time vacationing outdoors
